Museum Uetersen

Uetersen (ˈyːtɐzən) is a small city in the district of Pinneberg, in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. It is situated approx. 7 km (4 miles) south of Elmshorn, and 30 km (18 miles) northwest of Hamburg.

Mathias Rust took off from Uetersen for his historic flight in 1987.
